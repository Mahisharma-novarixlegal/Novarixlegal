About

The
Lawyer.

I am Mahi Sharma — a Delhi-based advocate with a practice that doesn't fit in one box. Copyright enforcement. Personality rights. Real estate. Criminal. Civil. I built it this way deliberately.

Most lawyers specialise early and stay narrow. I went the other direction — because the clients who need you most rarely have problems that fit neatly into one category. A rights dispute becomes a criminal matter. A property deal surfaces a regulatory issue. You need someone who can move across all of it.

I work with Media-Lexicon (Advocate Vishnu T. Menon, Defence Colony) for litigation, serve as Director of Strategic Alliances & Legal Affairs for CINEFIL, and advise Altera Realty on their Delhi NCR expansion. Three very different mandates. One common thread — someone who knows how Delhi actually works.
